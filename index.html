Wrap Up the Holidays with a Handmade Wreath!

In our Christian small group, making balsam fir wreaths adorned with pinecones, vibrant red berries, and a hand-crafted bow becomes a heartwarming way to strengthen our existing friendships and open the door to new ones.

As we gather around a table, the rich scent of greenery fills the air, sparking laughter and conversation. Each pinecone and berry we choose tells a story, while learning to create a unique Christmas bow reflects our shared creativity and holiday spirit.

In these moments, we share not only our artistic visions but also bond over stories, prayer, and the joy of giving that extends beyond the wreath-making itself. The finished wreath, a beautiful symbol of our time together, serves as a reminder of the friendships we nurtured amidst the greenery and festive spirit.

Each person leaves with a uniquely crafted wreath, perfect for hanging on the front door or gifting to that special friend.
